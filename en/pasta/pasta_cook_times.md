2 to 3 minutes

1 1/2 to 2 minutes

1 1/2 to 2 minutes

### Tagliatelle ###
1 1/2 to 2 minutes

### Ravioli ###
6 to 8 minutes

### Tortellini ###
8 to 10 minutes
