# Cheese Biscuits #

## Ingredients ##
```
2 cups flour
1/2 tsp salt
3/4 cup milk
3 tspns baking powder
1/4 cup shortening
3/4 cup grated cheese
```

### Instructions ###
1. Sift dry ingredients.
2. Cut in shortening and cheese.
3. Add milk.
4. Turn dough out on lightly floured surface, pat to 1/2" thick.
5. Cut with 2" cutter.
6. Baste with butter.
7. Bake at 450 F for 10 to 12 minutes.
